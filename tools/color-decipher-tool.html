How to Decipher your Personal Colors

So you've received your personal color, now what?

The color you’ve just called to yourself is your color for the day. It’s not any ordinary color, it is just for you and it contains information that will help guide you along your best path. It is just one of many tools you can carry with you on your everyday quest for enlightenment.

Colors elicit an emotional response, we’ve all experienced this and often dress based on our current moods. It is important to realize that our color preferences directly correspond with our Mental, Physical, Emotional and Spiritual states so that we may assess ourselves accordingly.

Select the color that best represents what you received using our Color Tool.

You’ll receive a phrase containing a key element and a question to help you focus your thoughts on exploring that key element.

It is important not to dwell on these phrases and questions. They are just guides to help you focus your intention.

You may pick more than one if it's in between, but choose carefully, you can only hit "submit" once per session.
