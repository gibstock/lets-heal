Daily Color and Mantra Intention Tool

Open The Door

Directions

There are signs all around us guiding us along our path if you know where to look. One simple tool is setting an intention to a color, paired with a mantra, and following that color when you encounter it out in the world.

The mantra will help guide you along your color path.

We recommend taking a screenshot of the color and mantra to take with you so you can keep this in your mind throughout your day in order to get the full effect of following your path.

Use the calming music to further relax you before or after your stressful day.
